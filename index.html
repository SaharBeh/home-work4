    • About Afghan Cities

    Kabul - The capital and largest city, Kabul is Afghanistan’s political, cultural, and economic center. It has a rich history dating back over 3,500 years.
    Kandahar – Known as a historical trade hub, it is culturally significant and was once the capital of the Afghan Empire under Ahmad Shah Durrani.
    Herat – Famous for its Persian-influenced architecture, Herat has been a key city along trade routes for centuries.
    Mazar-i-Sharif – Known for the Blue Mosque and as a major agricultural and commercial center.
    Jalalabad – Located near the Khyber Pass, it is an important trade and transit city with a warm climate and fertile land.
